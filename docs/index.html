Privacy Policy for Purrsuit

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

Important: Purrsuit is designed as an offline-first app. All your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Introduction

Purrsuit is an offline-first mobile app for tracking and managing pet encounters. Your privacy is our priority - we never collect, store, or transmit your personal data to external servers.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Photos and Pet Encounter Data

We store the following information locally on your device when you choose to create pet encounters:

  • Photos you capture (original and thumbnail versions stored locally)
  • Pet type (cat, dog, or other)
  • Mood tags, custom tags, and stickers you've added
  • Weather conditions and time of day (optional)
  • Optional notes you've written

2.2 Location Data (Optional)

Location data collection is entirely optional and under your control:

  • You can add GPS coordinates by granting location permission and choosing "Use Current Location"
  • You can add manual location labels (custom text descriptions)
  • You can skip location entirely for any encounter

Location data, when provided, is stored locally on your device only.

2.3 No Automatic Data Collection

We do not automatically collect any device information, usage statistics, or other data about your device or app behavior.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely within the app to:

  • Display your pet encounters with photos and information
  • Show encounter details, tags, and metadata you've added
  • Provide offline mapping when location data is included

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (MMKV). Your photos, encounter information, and any location data never leave your device. We do not use cloud storage, external databases, or any form of data transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Expo Platform Services

The app uses Expo's native modules for device functionality. These services operate locally on your device:

  • Camera access for photo capture (only when you choose to take photos)
  • Location services for GPS coordinates (only when you choose to add location)
  • File system access for storing photos locally on your device
  • Image processing for photo editing and thumbnail generation

5.2 Map Services

For offline-capable mapping, we use MapLibre with OpenFreeMap tiles. Map tiles are downloaded locally to your device for offline use. Your location data is never transmitted to mapping service providers.

5.3 Error Reporting

We use Sentry for crash reporting in production builds only. Error reports may include technical information about app crashes but do not include your personal data, photos, or encounter information. Reports are sent only when your device is online and only for debugging purposes to improve app stability.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • View your data: Access all stored encounters and photos within the app
  • Delete encounters: Remove individual encounters by long-pressing them in the app
  • Control permissions: Grant or deny camera/location access at any time through device settings
  • Delete all data: Uninstall the app to remove all stored data

7. Data Transmission

Purrsuit operates completely offline. No user data, photos, encounter information, or location data is transmitted to external servers. The app only downloads map tiles (stored locally) and may send anonymous error reports for debugging purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is designed for pet owners of all ages. We recommend parental guidance for users under 13. The app does not collect any personal information that would identify individual users.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete encounters or uninstall the app. We do not retain any data on external servers.
